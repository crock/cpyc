About Us

/ABOUT US

The Mission of the California Police Youth Charities is to build relationships between the law enforcement community and California youth. We will strive to provide opportunities for youth through various resources throughout the Sate of California and we will provide productive activites for "at risk" youth, run by positive adult role models.
